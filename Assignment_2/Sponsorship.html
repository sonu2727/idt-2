Sponsorship Opportunities

Runs for a Purpose is the only company dedicated to raising funds for various charities through virtual races that people can participate in from anywhere. Since starting in June 2020, we have raised over $350,000 for various charities. We offer a variety of sponsorship options that will give you months of continued exposure to these individuals.

Sponsorship Packages

Diamond Sponsor – $2,500

  • Logo prominently displayed on homepage with a link to your company website.
  • Logo placement on ALL digital marketing materials and campaigns.
  • Logo placed on race bibs and finisher certificates.
  • A special thank you in ALL email communication with logo and link to your website.
  • Dedicated sponsor email to all participants (post-race).
  • Dedicated flyer (4.125” x 3.375”) placed in all swag packages shipped to participants.
  • Dedicated post on social media sites (Facebook and Instagram).

Platinum Sponsor – $1,000

  • Logo prominently displayed on homepage with a link to your company website.
  • Logo placement on ALL digital marketing materials and campaigns.
  • Logo placed on race bibs and finisher certificates.
  • A special thank you in ALL email communication with logo and link to your website.
  • Coupon offer in registration confirmation emails to participants.
  • 5 Complimentary race entries.

Gold Sponsor – $500

  • Logo prominently displayed on all web pages with a link to your website.
  • Logo placement on ALL digital marketing materials.
  • A special thank you in ALL email communication with logo and link to your website.
  • Coupon offer in registration confirmation emails to participants.
  • 4 Complimentary race entries.
